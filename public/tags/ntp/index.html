ESXi Snapshot Problems: msg.snapshot.error-QUIESCINGERROR

Wed Feb 01, 2017 by cmohn in  VMware  ESXi, NTP, Snapshot, vSphere

[caption id=“attachment_4559” align=“alignright” width=“300”] Photo by Sonja Langford[/caption] Just a quick post about something I experienced at a client, with ESXi 6.0 hosts, today: If you have trouble performing VMware snapshots, and see a msg.snapshot.error-QUIESCINGERROR error, check the host time settings and NTP. In this case, snapshots of VMs located on other hosts in the cluster were fine, but once a VM was moved to the new host, snapshot operations failed after an hour or so. Read more →

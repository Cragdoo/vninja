Nordic VMUG Conference - My Thoughts

 Posted on December 4, 2013  |  3 minutes (487 words)

On Tuesday, December 3, 2013 VMware User Group Denmark arranged theNordic VMUG Conference. The event itself can only be described as a mini-VMworld, with over 600 registered attendees, and a speaker list worthy of such a nick-name. The opening keynote was held by Joe Baguley, the closing keynote was held by Chad Sakac. In between those two, you had the possibility to attend sessions held by Cormac Hogan, Mike Laverick, Frank Brix, Paudie O-Riordan, Frank Denneman, Mattias Sundling, David Davis and Duncan Epping.

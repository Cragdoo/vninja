Running Dockerflix on Ravello Systems

Thu Jan 14, 2016 by cmohn in  Virtualization  Docker, Dockerflix, Hipster IT, Ravello, VMware Photon

Dockerflix is a nice little project that allows you to route your Netflix (and other various streaming services) through a SNI Proxy to access content otherwise geo-blocked. Of course, this requires that you have a VM with for instance an US IP to provide the breakout network, and that’s where Ravello Systems comes into the equation.

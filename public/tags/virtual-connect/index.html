Virtual Connect FlexFabric and Direct-Attach FC 3Par Caveat

Thu May 30, 2013 by cmohn in  Storage  3Par, BladeSystem, Bug, Fibre Channel, HP, SFP+, storage, StoreServ, Virtual Connect

When configuring a new C7000 Blade Enclosure with a couple of FlexFabric 10Gb/24-port modules I ran into a rather annoying issue during setup. HP Virtual Connect 3.70 introduced support for Direct-Attach setups of HP 3Par StoreServ 7000 storage systems, where you can eliminate the need for dedicated FC switches. For full details, have a look at Implementing HP Virtual Connect Direct-Attach Fibre Channel with HP 3PAR StoreServ Systems.

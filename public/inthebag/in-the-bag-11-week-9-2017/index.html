In The Bag #11 - Week 9 2017

Technology

  • RVTools 3.9.2 (February, 2017) One of my favourite, and free, tools for VMware environments has been updated include support for vSphere 6.5.

  • Use LogInsight as your VMware Security Dashboard Edward Haletky has created a Log Insight content pack, with a security focus. This article by Christian Klose shows how you can use it to create a security dashboard. Good stuff!

  • Free ITIL training Perhaps not the most sexy topic out there, but some basic ITIL training is something everyone needs — Even if you don’t utilise in your organisation.

  • 360 Troubleshooting with vRealize Operations and vRealize Log Insight Good video from VMware, showing how Operations and Log Insight can work together.

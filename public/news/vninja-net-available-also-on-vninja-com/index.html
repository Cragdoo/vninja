vNinja.net available also on vNinja.com

Mon Mar 21, 2011 by cmohn in  News  site, vNinja

Thanks to the generosity of Todd Wright vNinja.net is now also available via vNinja.com. Todd came out of nowhere and offered to redirect his vNinja.com domain to vNinja.net, since he didn’t have time to do anything with it himself.

I’m very grateful that Todd wanted to do this, and thanks to some quick Apache trickery .com now redirects to .net to make sure Google doesn’t find and penalize any double content.

Thanks again Todd, it’s much appreciated!

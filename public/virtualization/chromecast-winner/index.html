And the Chromecast Winner is ...

A little over a month ago I announced the Want to Win a Google Chromecast  contest and finally the winner has been selected. Since there was a grand total of 5 submissions, and one of them was immediately disqualified for a distinct lack of Ninja-presence, I decided to just do a random draw.

The submitted photos were numbered by the order they appeared in on my post as I took this screenshot. I then put the number range 1 to 4 into random.org and recorded the output

[video width=«960» height=«540» mp4=«http://vninja.net/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Chromecast-Winner.mp4"][/video]

By random selection, the winning submission is Ninja with Entourage submitted by Chad Singleton.

Congratulations! Please contact me for shipping details, and I’ll get the Chromecast sponsored by Veeam shipped in your direction as soon as possible!
